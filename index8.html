Scenario 8: Radioactive Decay

System: Unstable nucleus, daughter nucleus and alpha particle
Initial State: An unstable stationary nucleus just before radioactive decay.
Final State: A daughter nucleus and an alpha particle were emitted and travelled in different directions. Gamma radiation was also emitted.
Remarks: Nil
Hint 1
Consider the principle of conservation of energy.
Hint 2
Consider the type of energy transfer when the gamma radiation is emitted.
Hint 3
Consider the type of energy associated to the nuclei and particle moving in different directions.
